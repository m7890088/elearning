Save the meal

What is our app about?

Save the Meal is a cell phone application that helps the customers connect to the people in need and victims of poverty. Our app contains two functions, the first one is where you can sell food supplies for lower prices to help them manage financially, and the second one is where you can donate supplies to them. The application is also connected to an organization where it hands out food twice a week in each branch in every city in Jordan.

Learn More

Dinners organized with the help of an organization

To try and help everybody as much as we could . We will connect with an organization to help us give food to people who are unable to reach us throughout our app. From 2-3 times a week the organization will take pre-prepared food from the app through us and give to the people unable to reach us.

The dinners organized by individual

We take leftover food from dinners and parties organized by individuals and check the food quality for it to be packed and sent to people in need. The packages should be sanitized. The packages should be sorted according to the type of food The food should be edible The packages should be dated

The Food Bank

People who have extra food can upload their items, for free or for a lower price into a basket. They may use their laptops, computers, and cell phones after providing their information. Each user is allowed to order only two meals per person everyday.

Food Sold Lower Than the Market Price

- Some may purchase extra food that will be stored for a few days and will be thrown away eventually. - "Save the meal " will prevent this from happening. People can sell the food for a lower price in order to support the people who are in need . Both sides will benefit.

THE FOOD THAT IS LISTED FOR FREE

Of course, in Save the Meal there is a whole section for free food dedicated to people who cannot afford it so as to help them get good food quality for their daily need. People can donate extra food by uploading it to the app. In that way the food will not expire and not go to waste . the people who get the donation will have good food quality food to eat.

Summary

In summary, our app will prevent a large amount of food from going to waste. Instead it will give this extra food to the less fortunate, who may not have enough to eat. Also, with the help of an organization whom is willing to fund our app, we will be able to turn our idea into reality. In addition to that, Food will be listed on the app for cheap prices or even for free to help with peoples unfortunate circumstances. Finally, if our dreams come into fruition, we may be able to make a better world for thousands of people.
